- `db` means “**d**efine **b**yte”, i.e. a reservation of **1** byte of memory,
- `dw` means “**d**efine **w**ord” (**2** bytes of memory),
- `dd` means “**d**efine **d**ouble-word” (**4** bytes of memory).
