I think I've figured out why I dislike committing to movie nights: I'm not sure if they'll be a good use of time

What makes a successful (at the end of the spectrum) movie night:
- everyone *wants* to watch a movie with each other, e.g participants as nodes and inclusion as edges form a complete graph
- everyone has a *reasonable* agreement to watch *the movie*
