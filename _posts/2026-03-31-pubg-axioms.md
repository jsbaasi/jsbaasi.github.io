- Right click weapon from ground, drag loot the ammo, start reloading, drag to weapon slot 2, animation cancel when ammo count updates
- Turn the wheels when driving over the apex of a hill/bump
- Aim for centre of mass
- Play death-match and warm-up spray on training range
- Take buildings when advancing ground otherwise you have no control
- Hear audio cues before pushing people
- Do not over-peek or stand in the same place for too long - be dynamic
- Do not face-check cornered players, bait/confuse before challenging
- Bait behind cover to put off an opponent's recoil control
- Watch flanks for your squad unless you're taking point
- You must move extremely slowly if you're lurking
- Take into account bowls/defensible positions and opposition cues/flight path/previous zones when evaluating positions
- Watch kill feed for information, especially in hot-drop starts
- Do not gamble with lethal grenades, use them to clear immediate space lest you're surprised
- Do not peek around smokes they're client-side
